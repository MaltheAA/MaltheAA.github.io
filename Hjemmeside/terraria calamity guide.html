how to beat terraria calamity

so you're wondering how to beat terraria calamity? well i'll show you!!


here we go

  1. name your character jetstream sam. this will ensure you can make it to moon lord very easily.
  2. get a bit of gear, lucky horseshoe, hermes boots and cloud in a bottle are good. a good weapon is also useful
  3. craft a bed
  4. get a gold/platinum pickaxe, not necessary but a good idea
  5. make a hellevator
  6. speedrun through the right side of hell until you reach the hell biocenter lab
  7. build a little hut outside with the bed to respawn there if you die
  8. infiltrate the lab from the side and take out the turrets either by imprisoning them or destroying them with a gold/platinum pickaxe
  9. open the chests and get the murasama
  10. easy game
  11. beat bosses and follow the wiki.gg calamity class setup and use the melee gear
